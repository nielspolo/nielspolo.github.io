Why Corporate Polo

Team Building That Actually Stands Out

Polo brings people together in a way a standard team-building day can't. It requires communication, trust, and quick thinking, all wrapped in a setting that feels exclusive and memorable. Niels has run these sessions for years, guiding groups through the basics of horse control and mallet work before building toward light-hearted, competitive play.

Introductory taster sessions for complete beginners
Structured team-building formats focused on communication and strategy
Private group bookings for clients, partners, or staff
Flexible group sizes with all equipment and horses provided
