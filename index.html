About Niels

30 Years of Polo.
One Passion.

Niels van Baasbank is a Dutch polo professional with over 30 years of international coaching and club management experience, including 20 years based in Dubai. Currently Head Coach at Desert Palm Polo Club, he brings a rare combination of playing excellence and coaching expertise.

His career spans founding the first polo academy in the Netherlands, managing Emaar's flagship Dubai Polo & Equestrian Club, and coaching hundreds of students from complete beginners to competitive players.

"Polo is not just a sport — it is a conversation between horse and rider, strategy and instinct, discipline and freedom."

HPA Level 2 Coach (2024)
HPA Overseas Polo Coach — renewed annually
Playing Handicap +1 (career high +3)
