Why Private Coaching

The Fastest Path to Real Improvement

Group lessons teach the fundamentals. Private coaching is where real transformation happens, because every minute of the session is spent entirely on you. Niels tailors each lesson to your mount, your swing, your positioning, and your mindset, adjusting his teaching style as your game evolves.

Whether you're a complete beginner or a competitive player chasing a handicap increase, sessions are structured around measurable improvement, not generic drills.

Mallet technique and swing mechanics
Horse control and communication
Positional play and game strategy
Mental preparation for competition
Personalised feedback between sessions

Who This Is For

Built Around Your Goals

Riders who are serious about progressing quickly, players preparing for tournaments, and anyone who wants coaching built entirely around their own weaknesses and schedule rather than a fixed class structure.

FAQ

Common Questions

How long is a private lesson?

Session length is flexible and discussed when you book, based on your goals and schedule.

How often should I train privately?

Many students train one to two times per week. Niels recommends a schedule based on your level and goals.

Can I combine private lessons with group clinics?

Yes, many students combine private 1-on-1 sessions with Stick & Ball Clinics or Team Training for a well-rounded program.
